What is IDTC?IDTC คืออะไร?

IDTC Energy Capital is a blockchain-based investment platform built on Polygon — the carbon-neutral, high-performance EVM chain. Our mission is to connect investors of all sizes with real, verified energy projects including solar farms, clean power infrastructure, and sustainable energy development across Asia and the Middle East.IDTC Energy Capital คือแพลตฟอร์มการลงทุนบนบล็อกเชน สร้างบน Polygon — เชนที่เป็น carbon-neutral และมีประสิทธิภาพสูง พันธกิจของเราคือเชื่อมโยงนักลงทุนทุกระดับกับโครงการพลังงานจริง ทั้งฟาร์มโซลาร์ โครงสร้างพลังงานสะอาด และการพัฒนาพลังงานยั่งยืนในเอเชียและตะวันออกกลาง

Through the IDTC token, participants gain investment pool access, passive staking yields, governance rights, and a transparent on-chain record of every fund deployment.

Business Modelรูปแบบธุรกิจ

How the Platform Worksแพลตฟอร์มทำงานอย่างไร

1

Investor Onboards (KYC/AML)

Investors complete identity verification, connect their Polygon wallet (MetaMask / WalletConnect), and purchase IDTC during sale rounds or on DEX.

2

Energy Project Due Diligence

The IDTC team and DAO committee evaluate projects against financial, environmental, and risk criteria. Approved projects are listed on-platform with full documentation.

3

Token-Gated Investment Pool

IDTC holders stake tokens into project-specific pools. Smart contracts record proportional ownership and lock funds in multi-sig escrow until project milestones are verified.

4

Revenue Distribution On-Chain

Project revenues (solar PPA income, grid payments, oil royalties) are converted to USDT and distributed on-chain proportionally to stakers every 30 days — automatically.

5

Governance & Liquidity Exit

Token holders vote on new projects and protocol changes via DAO. Investors can exit positions via DEX trading at any time after individual vesting periods complete.

💡 Platform Revenue Streams

  • Platform fee: 1.5% on all investment pool entries
  • Performance fee: 10% of distributed project returns
  • DEX trading fees: 0.3% on all IDTC swaps (shared with LPs)
  • Governance fee: 100 IDTC per proposal (burned)

🔥 Deflationary Mechanism

  • 20% of platform fees used to buy back & burn IDTC tokens
  • All governance proposal fees (100 IDTC each) are burned
  • Target: burn 10% of total supply over 5 years

🤝 Energy Partnerships

Letters of Intent (LOIs) in progress with solar developers in Thailand and UAE. First verified project go-live planned Q4 2026. All partnerships disclosed publicly via smart contract events on PolygonScan.

Independent Smart Contract Audit

Multi-Signature Treasury

All platform funds held in a 3-of-5 multi-sig wallet. No single person can move treasury funds without majority approval.

Testnet-First Policy

All contracts deployed on Polygon Amoy testnet for minimum 30 days and stress-tested before mainnet launch.

100% Verified Source Code

Security Implementation

  • Reentrancy guards (OpenZeppelin ReentrancyGuard) on all value transfers

Regulatory Frameworkกรอบกฎระเบียบ

🏢 Legal Entity

IDTC Energy Capital will be incorporated as a formal legal entity prior to public fundraising. Jurisdiction under review — targeting Thailand, UAE, or Singapore to ensure full regulatory compliance in our primary markets.

🪪 KYC / AML Policy

All investors complete identity verification (KYC) and anti-money laundering (AML) screening before participating in any token sale. Sumsub (enterprise KYC provider) integrated into the platform for compliant onboarding.

⚖️ Token Classification

IDTC is designed and marketed as a Utility Token providing platform access, governance rights, and ecosystem utility — not as a security instrument or investment contract. Legal opinion obtained from qualified counsel in each target jurisdiction.

🌍 Restricted Jurisdictions

Residents and citizens of the United States, China, and other jurisdictions where token sales are restricted by applicable law are prohibited from participating in IDTC token sales. Geo-blocking and terms-of-service enforcement will be strictly implemented.

📋 Smart Contract Governance

All token sale, vesting, and treasury operations are governed by audited, time-locked smart contracts. No manual override is possible without multi-signature approval — ensuring no single party can misappropriate funds under any circumstances.

✅ Our Public Commitments

We commit to: publishing all audit reports publicly, maintaining a funded bug bounty program, transparent on-chain treasury viewable by all, 12-month liquidity lock post-listing, and monthly community updates on project deployment progress.
